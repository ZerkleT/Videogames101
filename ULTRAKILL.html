ULTRAKILL

MANKIND IS DEAD.

BLOOD IS FUEL.

HELL IS FULL.

Welcome to Ultrakill. A world where humans are extinct, god is dead, and machines are fueled off of blood. You play as V1, a wartime prototype built to kill. Without any organic life left on the surface, you and many other robots take the plunge deep into the depths of hell.

But before you can start your killing spree, you need a weapon. After all, what is a Vampiric killer robot without their sci-fi arsenal of weapons?

The first weapon you find is the revolver; specifically the piercer variant. But this isn't any ordinary revolver. It's a special kind of gun that fires microscopic shards of metal, with the cylinder of the gun holding the revolving batteries that shoot these shards.
